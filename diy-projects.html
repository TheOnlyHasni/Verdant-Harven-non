Safety First

👁️

Eye Protection

Always wear safety glasses when cutting, drilling, or hammering to protect from flying debris.

🎧

Hearing Protection

Use ear protection when operating loud power tools for extended periods.

🧤

Hand Protection

Wear gloves when handling rough materials, but avoid them around rotating tools.

🔄

Tool Awareness

Always read tool manuals and understand proper operation before use.
