Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit a project or story to be featured?

Absolutely! We love featuring stories and projects from our community. Please send us an email with the subject "Community Feature" and include some photos and a brief description of your project.

Do you offer one-on-one design consultations?

While we don't offer formal one-on-one consultations at the moment, our team is always happy to answer specific questions via email. For complex queries, we may refer you to one of our trusted partners.

How do you select the products you recommend?

Our recommendations are based on thorough research, hands-on testing, and community feedback. We prioritize products that are sustainable, well-made, and offer great value. We are not paid to endorse specific products.

I'm a beginner. Where should I start?

Welcome! We recommend starting with our "Beginner Guides" section, accessible from the footer. The "Tiered Plant Stand" DIY project and the "Beginner's Vegetable Garden" guide are excellent starting points.
