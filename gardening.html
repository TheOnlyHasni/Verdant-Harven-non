Know Your Soil

Understanding your soil type is the foundation of successful gardening. Test your soil's pH and texture to choose the right plants and amendments.

Water Wisely

Deep, infrequent watering encourages strong root systems. Water early in the morning to reduce evaporation and prevent fungal diseases.

Right Plant, Right Place

Match plants to their preferred light conditions. Observe how sunlight moves through your garden throughout the day before planting.

Timing is Everything

Learn your local frost dates and plant according to seasonal requirements. Some plants thrive in cool weather while others need warmth.
